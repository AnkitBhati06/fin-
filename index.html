Key Assumptions & Notes

  • Operational Phases: Aug-Sep are dedicated to tech development. App launch and marketing begin in October.
  • Team Salaries: The full development, design, and intern team is hired from January 2026. The total projected cost for Team Salaries is ₹37,90,000 for the full financial year. This is a significant part of the overall burn rate, with a major increase starting in January 2026. The breakdown is as follows: initial month intern _ some basic salary for founders totaling ₹30,000 per month throughout the entire year. New Hires: The full team is brought on from January 2026. This includes 2 App Developers at ₹80,000 each, 1 Backend Developer at ₹80,000, 1 web develper, 1 Machine Learning Developer at ₹1,50,000, 1 UI/UX Designer at ₹40,000, and 3 ML Interns at ₹10,000 each. This brings the monthly salary cost up to ₹4,90,000 from January onwards.
  • Marketing: Campaigns start in October. The Campus CEO program expands in Jan-Feb. A Social Media Manager is hired from January.The total projected spend on Marketing is ₹10,32,000. The marketing strategy is phased, with costs fluctuating significantly throughout the year based on different initiatives: Initial Phase (October-December): The team recruits 10 Campus CEOs at a monthly cost of ₹1,000 per person plus an incentive, along with a monthly ad budget of ₹25,000. This is the initial launch and user acquisition phase. Expansion Phase (January-February): This is a high-growth period where the number of Campus CEOs expands to 100 for two months. Additionally, college fest promotions begin with a monthly budget of ₹1L-1.5L, and a Social Media Manager is hired at ₹30,000 per month. Sustained Phase (March onwards): The Campus CEO program scales back, but the ad budget and Social Media Manager costs continue, along with reduced promotional activity.
  • AWS Costs: Stage 1 costs (Aug-Dec) and Stage 2 costs (Jan-Jul) are distributed monthly.Our AWS server costs are divided into two stages to accommodate growth: Stage 1: A total cost of ₹1,08,315 is allocated over the first five months (August to December), which breaks down to approximately ₹21,663 per month. Stage 2: A total cost of ₹17,03,160 is allocated over the remaining seven months (January to July), which comes out to approximately ₹2,43,309 per month.
  • AI/ML Costs: Gemini costs run Oct-Dec. GPU Training and initial AI hosting are Jan-Mar. Model hosting continues at a different rate for the final four months. Our AI/ML infrastructure has a phased budget to align with development and launch: Gemini Costs: We have a budget of ₹1,20,000 for the first three months of operation (October to December), with no cost in the initial development phase. GPU Training: A significant cost of ₹9,96,000 is set for a three-month GPU training period from January to March. AI Model Hosting & Inference: This cost is planned to increase over time. We have a budget of ₹4,00,000 for the January to March quarter and a cost of ₹66,800 per month for the remaining four months (April to July).
  • Legal & Compliance: A one-time fee of ₹50,000 is allocated in August for setup.
  • Other Charges: This category combines several operational expenses that are not directly tied to salaries or major server costs. The total cost for these "other charges" is ₹3,10,000. Here is the breakdown: Tech Tools & Infra: A fixed cost of ₹10,000 per month is allocated for essential software like Jira, Figma, and Slack. The total yearly cost for this is ₹1,20,000. Office/Operations Infra: A fixed cost of ₹5,000 per month is budgeted for co-working space and supplies. The yearly total is ₹60,000. Legal & Compliance: This is a one-time cost of ₹50,000 that is allocated at the beginning of the financial year in August for initial legal setup. Miscellaneous: A fixed cost of ₹15,000 per month is set aside for unforeseen expenses, team events, and other operational needs. The total yearly cost is ₹1,80,000.
